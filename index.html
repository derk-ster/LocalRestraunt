Our Story

Gathered around the hearth since 1998.

Ember & Olive began as a family kitchen rooted in slow-cooked traditions. Today, we pair heritage recipes with farmers-market freshness, welcoming guests to linger over thoughtfully prepared plates and warm hospitality.

From weekend brunch to candlelit dinners, our team crafts experiences that celebrate connection, comfort, and the joy of sharing good food.
